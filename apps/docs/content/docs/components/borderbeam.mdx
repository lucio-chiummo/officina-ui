`BorderBeam` is a bordered surface with a beam of light that continuously orbits
its edge — an eye-catching accent for upsell cards, active states, and hero
panels. Wrap your content as children and tune the beam with `size` (thickness
in px), `duration` (seconds per lap), and `color` (defaults to the accent
token). The beam is decorative and pauses under `prefers-reduced-motion`.

## Example

<Demo>
  <BorderBeamDemo />
</Demo>

```tsx
import { BorderBeam } from '@officina/ui';

<BorderBeam duration={6} size={2}>
  <h3>Pro plan</h3>
  <p>Everything in Free, plus unlimited projects.</p>
</BorderBeam>;
```
